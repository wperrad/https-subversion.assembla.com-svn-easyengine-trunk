The Genesis -- 4 bedrooms, 4 bathrooms and a 3 car garage spread across 3,174 square feet of living space built with versatility and comfort in mind. This Next Gen® floor plan from our Imperial Collection gives you a home within a home and the luxury of Everything’s Included® features throughout. Quartz countertops in the kitchen, a pavered lanai and driveway outside and much, much more! The Genesis is a Wi-Fi Certified Smart Home Design™ floorplan. All Lennar homes come with a one year home warranty, pre-wired security system, dedicated customer service program and 24-hour emergency service
